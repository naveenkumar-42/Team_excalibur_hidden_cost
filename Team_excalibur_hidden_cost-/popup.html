About Dark Patterns

Dark patterns are manipulative user interface designs crafted to trick users into doing things they might not want to do, such as making purchases or signing up for subscriptions unknowingly.

Our extension helps you stay informed and make informed decisions while shopping online, protecting you from falling victim to these unethical practices.
